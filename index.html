Research

My current research centers on the design and evaluation of networked graphics systems, with an emphasis on real-time and distributed rendering for XR platforms. I am particularly interested in alternative scene representations for streaming interactive 3D content that move beyond conventional video encoding. More broadly, my work spans visual computing systems, addressing challenges at the intersection of rendering, networking, and perception.

Some keywords that show up in my research: remote/split rendering, reprojection, image-based rendering, geometry streaming, motion-to-photon latency, disocclusion handling, Quality-of-Experience (QoE).
